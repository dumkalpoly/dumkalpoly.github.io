Training And Placement Cell

More about our Placements :

Dumkal Polytechnic embarked on its onward journey in the month of September 2002.Gradually the institute has evolved to be a leading center in the Technology Map of West Bengal for imparting quality education in Engineering & Technology curriculum according to rules, regulations and guidelines of AICTE and West Bengal State Council of Technical Education.

Like other wings of the institute the Training & Placement Cell is an object of pride to the students of this college. Job orientation program through Mock Interview, Aptitude Test, Personality Development, Group Discussion sessions etc. are done on regular basis.

Tie up with professional experts has been made. Students of Dumkal Polytechnic start with training from IBM , IIBM , HCL , MicroPro , IEM, ARDENT COMPUTER PVT. LTD., BSNL, DPL, IOC, DVC, WBSEDCL, WBPDCL , etc. and crack interviews in companies like ABB Ltd. , Synergy Multitech , Comtel India (P) , HCL , APTEL BUISNESS SOLUTION , ORBIT , iCONE & other renowned companies.

A Training and placement cell has been formed under close supervision of M. Alam Sirajee at the institute and Mr. A.K. Mondal at the city office. A tie up with professional experts has been made. Our Institute arranges grooming classes for personality development and to prepare them for proper placement. The students are immensely benefited from these special employability enhancement programmes to brush up their soft skills as well as technical skills. Nearly all the students, those who have passed from our institute, since its inception, have either been employed in well-known organizations or have gone for higher education.
